FAQ

This slider sucks, why does it jump from $0 to $45,045 to $112,613?

That's literally the width of one pixel at this scale and the slider's gotta fit all potential incomes within the supported range. Just type your income in the box if you want a super specific zero.

Yes, but AOC proposed a 70% marginal tax rate at income over $10,000,000. So the 70% kicks in above your ten-millionth dollar. So even if you make $10,000,001 in a year you'd only be paying 70 cents extra total in taxes that year under this fairly conservative proposal.
